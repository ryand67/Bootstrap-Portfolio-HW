About Me

Hi! I'm Ryan, born and raised in Charlotte, NC and I've been here ever since! I've been interested in music and computers all of my life. I've been playing guitar since I was 10 years old, and began to teach myself coding casually at 12. I've played with dozens of bands and churches since starting and released a handful of albums and EP's independently. I've recently enrolled in the UNCC Coding Bootcamp to try to shift careers from a full time musician to becoming a web developer. Very excited to see where this opportunity leads!
